Membership

Everyone is welcome to join the Urban Farm and to volunteer in our programs and at our events. Anyone wanting to have a gardening plot must become a member and pay the membership fee, as well as the garden plot fee. With membership you can participate in meetings, and help organize events at the Farm. Your contribution helps maintain this heritage farm in the community of Spryfield.

The Urban Farm has some garden plots which can be rented on a yearly basis. Some of these plots are part of the Come Grow With Us program. Some are for knowledgeable gardeners who want to grow food organically. Sign-up for the plots takes place in the Spring, at our Seedy Saturday and Plant Sale. Plot holders must contribute at least five hours every month to the general upkeep of the Urban Farm. The coordinator keeps a list of tasks to be performed by volunteers.
